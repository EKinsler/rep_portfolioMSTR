FLAGSTAFF
         

AFFORDABILITY & FINANCES


The mission of the Flagstaff Housing Authority is to assist low-income families with safe, decent, and affordable housing opportunities as they strive to improve the quality of their lives. The Housing Authority is committed to operating in an efficient, ethical, and professional manner. The Housing Authority will create and maintain partnerships with its clients and appropriate community organizations in order to accomplish this mission.



The City of Flagstaff Housing Authority will open the Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program waiting list. Families wishing to apply for the Section 8 Program must complete an on-line pre-application. Pre-Applications will be available online effective November 19, 2019 at 8:00 am. All applications must be filed electronically. The URL address for the WebApp Tool will be posted prior to November 19, 2019. Applicants are not required to come to the Housing Authority office to apply. Applicants will be able to use any computer, tablet or smart phone with internet access to apply, including free computers at public libraries, other social service agencies, or at the City of Flagstaff Housing Authority Office located at 3481 N. Fanning Dr. Flagstaff, AZ 86004 during typical business hours.
